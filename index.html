Bla bla HTML DOESN'T PROVIDE A HUGE NUMBER OF ELEMENTS for us to work with. The selection available is more like that of a corner store than a Walmart. We have paragraphs, lists, and headlines but we don't have events, news stories, or recipes. HTML gives us an element for marking up a string as an abbreviation, but it doesn't give us an element for marking up a number as a price. Clearly, this limitation hasn't been a show-stopper; just look at the amazing variety of websites out there. Even though HTML might not provide a specific element for marking up a particular piece of content, it provides just enough flexibility to be "good enough."
